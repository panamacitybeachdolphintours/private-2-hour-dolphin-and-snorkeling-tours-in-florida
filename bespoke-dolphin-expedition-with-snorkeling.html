Frequently Asked Questions

What is the natural enemy of dolphins?

Sharks are considered natural predators of dolphins, posing a threat to their survival in the wild.

What should you not do while swimming with dolphins?

While swimming with dolphins, do not chase, grab, or attempt to ride them. Respect their space and behavior.

What does a dolphin do every two hours?

Dolphins do not sleep like humans; instead, they rest one hemisphere of their brain at a time, with each side taking turns every few hours.

Do dolphins swim by cruise ships?

Dolphins are known to swim near cruise ships, especially in areas where dolphin populations are abundant.
