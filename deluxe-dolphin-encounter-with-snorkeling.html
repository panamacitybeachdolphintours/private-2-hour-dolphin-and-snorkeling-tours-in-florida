Frequently Asked Questions

What scares dolphins away?

Loud noises, sudden movements, and approaching too quickly can scare dolphins away. It's essential to maintain a quiet and respectful presence.

What beach in Florida has the most dolphins?

The Gulf Coast of Florida, including areas near Sarasota and Marco Island, is known for its high dolphin populations.

What to do if a dolphin approaches you?

If a dolphin approaches you, maintain a calm demeanor and avoid sudden movements. Enjoy the encounter from a respectful distance without attempting to touch or chase the dolphin.

Which beach is best for dolphin cruise?

Some of the best beaches for dolphin cruises include Clearwater Beach, Marco Island, and Sarasota.

Is it better to swim with dolphins in the morning or afternoon?

The morning may offer calmer seas and better lighting for dolphin encounters, but dolphins can be active throughout the day.
