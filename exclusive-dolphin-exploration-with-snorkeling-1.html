Frequently Asked Questions

Do you get wet on dolphin tours?

Yes, typically you may get splashed or wet during a dolphin tour, especially if the dolphins come close to the boat.

What to expect when you go swimming with dolphins?

When swimming with dolphins, expect a memorable and potentially exhilarating experience, but always prioritize the well-being and safety of the dolphins.

How much does it cost to swim with the dolphins at Dolphin Cove?

The cost to swim with dolphins at Dolphin Cove can vary depending on the location and type of experience, but prices generally range from $100 to $300 or more per person.

What not to do around dolphins?

Do not attempt to touch, feed, or swim with wild dolphins. Avoid making loud noises or sudden movements that could startle or stress them.

What is the natural enemy of dolphins?

Sharks are considered natural predators of dolphins, posing a threat to their survival in the wild.
