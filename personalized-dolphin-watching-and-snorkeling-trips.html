Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safer to swim with dolphins or sharks?

Swimming with dolphins is generally safer than swimming with sharks, as dolphins are not typically aggressive toward humans.

Is the Blue lagoon dolphin Encounter worth it?

The Blue Lagoon dolphin encounter can be worth it for those seeking a memorable experience with dolphins in a well-maintained and supervised environment.

Why can you not swim in the Blue Lagoon?

Swimming is prohibited in the Blue Lagoon to protect its delicate ecosystem and ensure the safety of visitors.

What is a dolphins worst enemy?

Dolphins face various threats in the wild, including pollution, habitat loss, and predation by sharks and other marine animals.

What scares dolphins away?

Loud noises, sudden movements, and approaching too quickly can scare dolphins away. It's essential to maintain a quiet and respectful presence.
