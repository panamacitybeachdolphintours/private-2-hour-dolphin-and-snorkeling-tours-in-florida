Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the best place for dolphin watching?

Some of the best places for dolphin watching include coastal areas with abundant dolphin populations, such as Hawaii, the Bahamas, and certain regions of Florida.

What month do dolphins have babies?

Dolphins typically give birth in warmer months, with peaks in baby dolphin sightings occurring in late spring and summer.

What should you wear when swimming with dolphins?

Wear a swimsuit, sunscreen, and a rash guard for sun protection. Avoid wearing loose jewelry or accessories that could pose a hazard.

What are dolphins worst enemies?

Natural enemies of dolphins include sharks, killer whales, and larger predatory fish.

Do you need water shoes to swim with dolphins?

Water shoes are recommended for swimming with dolphins to protect your feet from rough surfaces and potential hazards.
