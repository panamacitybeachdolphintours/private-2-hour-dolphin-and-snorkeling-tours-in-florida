Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to swim with dolphins in the morning or afternoon?

The morning may offer calmer seas and better lighting for dolphin encounters, but dolphins can be active throughout the day.

Are dolphin tours safe for dolphins?

Dolphin tours should be conducted responsibly to ensure the safety and well-being of dolphins. Reputable operators prioritize minimizing disturbance to dolphins and their habitats.

Do dolphins like swimming with humans?

Dolphins may enjoy swimming with humans if interactions are conducted respectfully and without causing stress or harm to the animals.

Where is the best place to see dolphins in the world?

Locations like the Bahamas, Hawaii, and the Galapagos Islands are renowned for offering unforgettable dolphin encounters.

What percentage of people get seasick on a cruise?

Seasickness affects around 30% of cruise passengers, but the severity can vary depending on factors like weather conditions and individual susceptibility.
